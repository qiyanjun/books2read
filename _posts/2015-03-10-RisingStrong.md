### Rising Strong 
- By: Brené Brown
- [URL](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/23317538-rising-strong)
-  This book is  about surviving hurt, acknowledging shame, embracing vulnerability, learning how to tell our stories, and getting back up to do it all over again, with courage and determination. 
- The author encourages readers to embrace vulnerability and transform failure and shame through a simple process of re-evaluating the stories we tell ourselves.
